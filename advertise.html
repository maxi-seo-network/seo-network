Frequently Asked Questions

How does sponsored content work?

Sponsored content is paid editorial that we write about your software. We maintain editorial integrity while highlighting your product's features, benefits, and use cases. All sponsored content is clearly labeled with a "Sponsored" badge.

Will sponsored content affect my SEO?

No. All sponsored content includes proper disclosure and nofollow links where appropriate. This protects both your brand and our site from SEO penalties. Google's guidelines allow sponsored content when properly disclosed.

Can I approve the content before publication?

Yes. All packages include at least one round of revisions. You'll receive a draft for review, can request changes, and approve the final content before it goes live.

How long does a sponsored review stay on your site?

Permanently. Unlike banner ads that disappear when you stop paying, sponsored reviews remain on our site indefinitely, providing ongoing SEO value and traffic.

Do you guarantee positive reviews?

No. We maintain editorial integrity and provide honest assessments. However, we work with you to highlight your product's strengths and ensure accuracy. Our readers trust us because we're objective.

What's the difference between sponsored and featured content?

Sponsored content: You pay for placement, we write the review, includes proper disclosure.

Featured content: Your existing review gets premium placement on homepage and category pages for a limited time.

Can I provide my own content?

We prefer to write all content to maintain quality and editorial standards. However, you can provide product information, key features, and messaging points for us to incorporate.

How do I get started?

Email us at advertise@toolreviewshub.com with your product details. We'll review your software, suggest the best package, and provide a quote within 24 hours.
