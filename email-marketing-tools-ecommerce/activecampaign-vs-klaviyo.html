ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo: Which is Better for E-commerce? (2026)

Published: March 1, 2026 | Updated: March 18, 2026 | Tools Reviews Hub Team

📋 Key Takeaways

  • Winner for pure e-commerce: Klaviyo (deeper Shopify integration, purpose-built)
  • Winner for CRM + email: ActiveCampaign (includes CRM, sales automation)
  • Pricing (1,000 contacts): ActiveCampaign $29/mo vs Klaviyo $30/mo (nearly identical)
  • Free plan: Klaviyo offers 250 contacts free; ActiveCampaign has 14-day trial only
  • Best for: Klaviyo = Shopify/WooCommerce stores; ActiveCampaign = B2B + B2C, CRM needs

Quick Summary: ActiveCampaign vs Klaviyo

Both ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo are powerful email marketing platforms, but they serve different needs. Klaviyo was built specifically for e-commerce with deep Shopify and WooCommerce integration. ActiveCampaign is a broader marketing automation platform that includes CRM and sales automation.

Important Pricing Notes

  • ActiveCampaign Lite: Starts at $29/mo (1,000 contacts) but lacks e-commerce automation. You need Plus ($49/mo) for e-commerce features.
  • Klaviyo Free: Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month. Great for testing.
  • SMS: Both charge extra for SMS (~$0.01/segment).
  • CRM: ActiveCampaign includes CRM; Klaviyo doesn't have one (would need separate tool).

💡 Value consideration: If you need CRM, ActiveCampaign is better value (email + CRM in one). If you don't need CRM, Klaviyo's free plan and e-commerce focus make it the winner.

Migration: Switching Between Platforms

ActiveCampaign → Klaviyo

  1. Export contacts from ActiveCampaign (CSV)
  2. Import to Klaviyo
  3. Set up Klaviyo integration with Shopify
  4. Recreate automation flows (many have templates)
  5. Run parallel for 1-2 weeks
  6. Cancel ActiveCampaign

Time: 1-2 weeks | Difficulty: Medium

Klaviyo → ActiveCampaign

  1. Export contacts from Klaviyo
  2. Import to ActiveCampaign
  3. Set up ActiveCampaign integration

  5. Recreate automations (more manual work)

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better for e-commerce: ActiveCampaign or Klaviyo?

For pure e-commerce, Klaviyo is better. It's purpose-built for online stores with deeper Shopify integration, better product recommendations, and specialized e-commerce flows. ActiveCampaign is better if you need email + CRM in one tool, have B2B sales alongside B2C, or want more general marketing automation beyond e-commerce.

Is Klaviyo cheaper than ActiveCampaign?

At small contact counts, Klaviyo and ActiveCampaign are similar. For 1,000 contacts: Klaviyo $30/mo vs ActiveCampaign $29/mo (Lite) or $49/mo (Plus for e-commerce). However, Klaviyo's free plan (250 contacts) is more generous than ActiveCampaign's 14-day trial. At 10,000 contacts, both are around $150/mo. ActiveCampaign can become more expensive when you need higher-tier plans for CRM features.

Can you use ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo together?

Yes, some businesses use both. You might use Klaviyo for e-commerce email (abandoned cart, welcome series, product recommendations) and ActiveCampaign for sales automation, B2B leads, and CRM. However, this doubles your costs and complexity. Most businesses should pick one primary platform.

Does ActiveCampaign integrate with Shopify?

Yes, ActiveCampaign has a native Shopify integration available in the Shopify App Store. It syncs customer data, orders, and products. However, Klaviyo's Shopify integration is deeper with more events tracked (like product views and browsing behavior). ActiveCampaign's integration is good; Klaviyo's is excellent.

Does Klaviyo have a CRM?

No, Klaviyo does not have a built-in CRM. It's purely an email and SMS marketing platform. If you need CRM functionality, you'd need to integrate with a separate tool like HubSpot, Pipedrive, or use ActiveCampaign which includes CRM. For pure e-commerce stores that don't need B2B sales tracking, Klaviyo's lack of CRM isn't usually a problem.

Final Verdict

🏆 Our Recommendation

For Pure E-commerce Stores:

Choose Klaviyo. It's purpose-built for e-commerce, has the deepest Shopify integration, and offers a generous free plan to start. The pre-built flows and revenue attribution make it the clear winner for Shopify/WooCommerce stores.

For E-commerce + B2B / Sales Teams:

Choose ActiveCampaign. The built-in CRM, sales automation, and flexible workflows are perfect for businesses that sell both B2C online and B2B to wholesale customers, or have sales teams that need pipeline tracking.

For Budget-Conscious Beginners:

Start with Klaviyo's free plan (250 contacts). You can always migrate later.

For Growing E-commerce (10k+ contacts):

Both platforms are similarly priced (~$150/mo). Klaviyo remains better for pure e-commerce. ActiveCampaign becomes attractive if you want CRM included.
