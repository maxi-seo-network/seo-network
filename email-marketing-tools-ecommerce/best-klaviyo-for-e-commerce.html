What is Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is an email and SMS marketing platform built specifically for e-commerce. Unlike general-purpose email tools, Klaviyo was designed from the ground up to integrate with online stores, making it the go-to choice for Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce merchants who want to drive revenue through email.

What sets Klaviyo apart is its deep data integration. It doesn't just send emails—it tracks customer behavior across your store, segments based on purchase history, browsing patterns, and engagement, and automates personalized messages at scale. For stores doing $50k+ in annual revenue, Klaviyo typically generates 20-30% of total revenue.

Quick verdict: Klaviyo is the best choice for e-commerce-focused email marketing. If you run a Shopify store and care about email revenue, this is your platform. For businesses needing CRM + email in one tool, consider ActiveCampaign or HubSpot.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo free for e-commerce?

Yes, Klaviyo offers a free plan for up to 250 contacts and 500 email sends per month. This is ideal for new e-commerce stores testing the platform. The free plan includes core email features, basic automation, and Shopify/WooCommerce integration.

How much does Klaviyo cost per month?

Klaviyo pricing starts free for up to 250 contacts. Paid plans start at $20/month for 500 contacts, $60/month for 1,500 contacts, $150/month for 10,000 contacts, and scale up based on contact count. SMS is priced separately at ~$0.01 per segment.

Is Klaviyo better than Mailchimp for e-commerce?

For e-commerce specifically, Klaviyo is generally better than Mailchimp. Klaviyo was built for e-commerce with native Shopify/WooCommerce integration, abandoned cart flows, and product recommendations. Mailchimp is more general-purpose and less e-commerce focused. However, Mailchimp's free plan supports more contacts (500 vs 250).

Does Klaviyo work with Shopify?

Yes, Klaviyo has the deepest Shopify integration of any email platform. It syncs customer data, order history, products, and browsing behavior in real-time. The integration is free and available through the Shopify App Store with 5-minute setup.

Can you use Klaviyo for SMS marketing?

Yes, Klaviyo offers SMS marketing as an add-on to email plans. SMS pricing is based on segments sent (approximately $0.01 per segment). You can create SMS-only flows, email+SMS combined flows, or let customers choose their preferred channel. SMS is particularly effective for time-sensitive offers and shipping notifications.
