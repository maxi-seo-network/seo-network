Why Email Marketing Matters for Shopify

For Shopify store owners, email marketing isn't optional—it's one of the most profitable marketing channels available. With $36 return for every $1 spent, email outperforms social media, paid ads, and SEO in ROI for most e-commerce stores.

Here's why email marketing is essential for Shopify:

  • Own your audience: Unlike social media followers, you own your email list
  • Higher conversion rates: Email converts at 2-5% vs 0.5-1% for social
  • Automated revenue: Flows like abandoned cart recovery work 24/7
  • Customer retention: 40% of revenue comes from repeat customers (email drives retention)
  • Cost-effective: Free to $100/month for most stores—much cheaper than paid ads

This guide covers everything you need to set up email marketing for your Shopify store, from choosing the right app to building automated flows that generate revenue.

How to Set Up Klaviyo for Shopify (Step-by-Step)

Klaviyo is the most popular email marketing app for Shopify. Here's how to set it up:

Step 1: Create Your Klaviyo Account

  1. Go to Klaviyo's website and sign up
  2. Enter your Shopify store URL when prompted
  3. Klaviyo will detect Shopify and start the integration

Step 2: Install the Klaviyo App from Shopify

  1. Go to Shopify Admin → Apps
  2. Search for "Klaviyo"
  3. Click "Add app" and authorize the connection
  4. Klaviyo will automatically sync your products, customers, and orders

Step 3: Enable Data Sync

  1. In Klaviyo, go to Integrations → Shopify
  2. Enable the following syncs:
    • ✓ Customers
    • ✓ Orders
    • ✓ Products
    • ✓ Inventory
  3. Wait 24-48 hours for full historical data sync

Step 4: Set Up Your First Flow

  1. Go to Flows → Create Flow
  2. Choose "Abandoned Cart" template
  3. Customize email templates with your branding
  4. Set timing: Email 1 at 1 hour, Email 2 at 24 hours, Email 3 at 72 hours
  5. Preview with test emails
  6. Click "Activate" to go live

Step 5: Add Klaviyo Signup Forms to Shopify

  1. In Klaviyo, go to Signup Forms → Create New
  2. Choose a popup, flyout, or embedded form
  3. Customize design and targeting rules
  4. Klaviyo will automatically embed the form on your Shopify store
  5. Test by visiting your store as a new visitor

Setup time: 1-2 hours for basic setup, 1 day for full configuration

How to Set Up Shopify Email (Built-In Option)

Shopify Email is free and built into your store. Here's how to set it up:

Step 1: Access Shopify Email

  1. Go to Shopify Admin → Marketing
  2. Click "Create campaign" → "Shopify Email"
  3. Accept terms to activate

Step 2: Create Your First Campaign

  1. Choose a template or start from scratch
  2. Customize with your branding and products
  3. Add products directly from your Shopify catalog
  4. Preview on desktop and mobile
  5. Schedule or send immediately

Step 3: Set Up Automation

  1. Go to Marketing → Automations
  2. Choose from templates:
    • Welcome series
    • Abandoned cart
    • Post-purchase follow-up
    • Win-back
  3. Customize email content and timing
  4. Activate automation

Step 4: Add Signup Form

  1. Go to Online Store → Themes → Customize
  2. Add "Newsletter" section to your footer or homepage
  3. Connect to Shopify Email list
  4. Save and publish

Limitations: Shopify Email lacks advanced segmentation, product recommendations, and A/B testing available in Klaviyo.

Essential Email Flows for Shopify Stores

Set up these five automated flows to generate revenue on autopilot:

1. Welcome Series (Must-Have)

Trigger: New subscriber or first order

Emails: 3-5 emails over 7 days

Revenue Impact: 3-5x higher conversion than campaigns

Recommended Structure:

  • Email 1 (Immediate): Welcome + 10% discount code
  • Email 2 (Day 2): Brand story + bestseller products
  • Email 3 (Day 4): Social proof + customer reviews
  • Email 4 (Day 7): Limited-time offer + urgency

2. Abandoned Cart Recovery (Critical)

Trigger: Cart created, no purchase in 1 hour

Emails: 3 emails

Revenue Impact: Recovers 10-15% of lost sales

Recommended Structure:

  • Email 1 (1-2 hours): Friendly reminder, no discount
  • Email 2 (24 hours): Social proof + benefits
  • Email 3 (48-72 hours): 10-15% discount offer

3. Post-Purchase Follow-Up

Trigger: Order completed

Emails: 3-4 emails

Revenue Impact: Increases repeat purchase rate by 20-30%

Recommended Structure:

  • Email 1 (Immediate): Order confirmation
  • Email 2 (When shipped): Shipping notification + tracking
  • Email 3 (7-14 days): How to use + cross-sell
  • Email 4 (21-30 days): Review request

4. Browse Abandonment

Trigger: Viewed product, no purchase

Emails: 1-2 emails

Revenue Impact: Captures customers earlier in funnel

5. Win-Back Campaign

Trigger: No purchase in 60-90 days

Emails: 1-3 emails

Revenue Impact: Reactivates 5-10% of dormant customers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best email marketing app for Shopify?

Klaviyo is the best email marketing app for Shopify stores due to its native integration, real-time data sync, and built-in e-commerce flows. Shopify Email is a good free alternative for new stores, while ActiveCampaign is ideal for stores needing CRM features alongside email marketing.

Does Shopify have built-in email marketing?

Yes, Shopify Email is Shopify's built-in email marketing app. It's free for up to 10,000 emails/month on Basic and higher plans and includes templates, automation, and basic segmentation. However, it lacks the advanced features of dedicated platforms like Klaviyo or ActiveCampaign.

How do I connect Klaviyo to Shopify?

To connect Klaviyo to Shopify: 1) Create a Klaviyo account, 2) Go to Integrations → Shopify, 3) Enter your Shopify store URL, 4) Authorize the connection, 5) Enable data sync for orders, products, and customers. The integration takes 24-48 hours for full data sync and requires no coding.

How much does email marketing cost for Shopify stores?

Email marketing costs for Shopify stores range from free (Shopify Email on Basic+ plans, Klaviyo free tier for up to 500 contacts) to $60-300/month for growing stores. Typical costs: $0-50/month for 0-1,000 contacts, $50-150/month for 1,000-5,000 contacts, $150-300/month for 5,000-10,000 contacts.

What email flows should every Shopify store have?

Every Shopify store should have these essential email flows: 1) Welcome series for new subscribers, 2) Abandoned cart recovery (3 emails), 3) Post-purchase thank you, 4) Order confirmation/shipping, 5) Product review requests, 6) Win-back campaigns for inactive customers. These flows typically generate 15-30% of e-commerce revenue.
