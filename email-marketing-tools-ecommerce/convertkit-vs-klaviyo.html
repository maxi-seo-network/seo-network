ConvertKit vs Klaviyo: Which Email Tool Is Better for Creators in 2026?

Last updated: March 2026 | 14 min read

📋 Quick Verdict

  • Best for creators: ConvertKit — built for bloggers, course creators, and digital product sellers
  • Best for e-commerce: Klaviyo — Shopify integration, product recommendations, revenue tracking
  • Best price: ConvertKit ($15/mo) is cheaper for creator lists
  • Best free plan: ConvertKit (1,000 subscribers) vs Klaviyo (250 contacts)
  • Best automation: Klaviyo for e-commerce flows, ConvertKit for creator funnels

Overview: ConvertKit vs Klaviyo

ConvertKit and Klaviyo serve different markets. ConvertKit is built for creators — bloggers, YouTubers, podcasters, course creators, and digital product sellers. Klaviyo is built for e-commerce — Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce stores.

While they both send emails, their strengths are very different. ConvertKit excels at creator funnels, tagging, and selling digital products. Klaviyo excels at e-commerce automation, product recommendations, and revenue tracking.

Feature Comparison

For Creators (ConvertKit Strengths)

Visual Automation Builder: ConvertKit's automation is designed for creator funnels — welcome sequences, course launches, webinar signups, product launches. The visual builder makes complex funnels easy.

Tag-Based Segmentation: ConvertKit uses simple tags instead of complex lists. Tag subscribers by interest, purchase history, or engagement level.

Landing Pages: ConvertKit includes beautiful landing page templates designed for creators — webinar signups, course enrollments, lead magnets.

Creator Commerce: Built-in product sales, course hosting, and community features. Sell digital products directly from ConvertKit.

For E-commerce (Klaviyo Strengths)

Shopify Integration: Klaviyo integrates deeply with Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce. Sync customer data, order history, and product catalog automatically.

Product Recommendations: Klaviyo shows personalized product recommendations in emails based on browsing history and purchase behavior.

Abandoned Cart: Advanced abandoned cart flows with product images, dynamic discounts, and multi-step sequences.

Revenue Tracking: See exactly how much revenue each email and flow generates. Track customer lifetime value.

When to Choose Each Tool

Choose ConvertKit If:

  • You're a blogger, podcaster, or YouTuber
  • You sell digital products or courses
  • You want landing pages included
  • You're building a creator funnel
  • You have under 10,000 subscribers
  • You sell webinars, courses, or memberships

Choose Klaviyo If:

  • You run a Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce store
  • You sell physical products

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertKit better than Klaviyo?

ConvertKit is better for creators selling digital products, courses, and memberships. Klaviyo is better for e-commerce stores selling physical products. Choose based on your business model.

Which is cheaper: ConvertKit or Klaviyo?

ConvertKit is cheaper for creator lists. At 10,000 subscribers, ConvertKit is $100/month vs Klaviyo's $150/month. However, Klaviyo's revenue tracking often pays for itself for e-commerce.

Can I use ConvertKit for e-commerce?

ConvertKit works for e-commerce but lacks Klaviyo's deep Shopify integration, product recommendations, and revenue tracking. For serious e-commerce, Klaviyo is superior. For digital products and courses, ConvertKit excels.

Can I switch from ConvertKit to Klaviyo?

Yes, you can migrate from ConvertKit to Klaviyo. Both offer data export. Klaviyo has migration tools and support. Plan 1-2 weeks for full migration and flow recreation.
