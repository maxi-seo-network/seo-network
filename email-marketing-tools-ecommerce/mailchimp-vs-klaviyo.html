Mailchimp vs Klaviyo: Which Email Marketing Tool Is Better in 2026?

Last updated: March 2026 | 12 min read

📋 Quick Verdict

  • Choose Klaviyo if: You run an e-commerce store (Shopify, WooCommerce) and need deep integration, product recommendations, and revenue tracking.
  • Choose Mailchimp if: You're a small business, beginner, or need general email marketing without e-commerce complexity.
  • Price winner: Mailchimp is cheaper at most contact levels (free plan to 500 contacts).
  • E-commerce winner: Klaviyo by far — built specifically for online stores.

Overview: Mailchimp vs Klaviyo

Mailchimp and Klaviyo are two of the most popular email marketing platforms, but they serve different audiences. Mailchimp is a general-purpose email marketing tool popular with small businesses and beginners. Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce with deep Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce integrations.

Feature Comparison

Email Automation

Mailchimp: Good automation with visual builder. Supports welcome series, abandoned cart (on Standard+), and behavioral triggers. Limited to basic e-commerce flows.

Klaviyo: Excellent automation built for e-commerce. Advanced flows for abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase, win-back, VIP programs. Visual flow builder is more sophisticated.

E-commerce Integration

Mailchimp: Basic Shopify/WooCommerce integration. Syncs orders and customer data. Product recommendations available only on Premium plan.

Klaviyo: Deep native integration with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento. Real-time sync of orders, products, customer behavior. Built-in product recommendations, revenue tracking, and customer lifetime value.

Segmentation

Mailchimp: Good segmentation based on demographics, behavior, and purchase history. Limited to 5 conditions on free plan.

Klaviyo: Advanced segmentation using 100+ data points including purchase history, browsing behavior, customer lifetime value, and predictive analytics.

Analytics & Reporting

Mailchimp: Standard open rates, click rates, unsubscribe tracking. Revenue tracking limited to Premium.

Klaviyo: Comprehensive e-commerce analytics: revenue per email, per campaign, per flow. Customer lifetime value, predictive analytics, cohort analysis.

When to Choose Each Platform

Choose Mailchimp If:

  • You're a small business without an online store
  • You're just starting with email marketing
  • You need a website builder included
  • Budget is your primary concern
  • You send newsletters more than e-commerce flows
  • You need strong social media integration

Choose Klaviyo If:

  • You run a Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce store
  • Email drives significant revenue for your business
  • You need advanced abandoned cart automation
  • You want product recommendations in emails
  • You need customer lifetime value tracking
  • You're scaling past 10,000 contacts

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Klaviyo better than Mailchimp for e-commerce?

Yes, Klaviyo is generally better for e-commerce due to its deep Shopify integration, product recommendations, and revenue tracking. Mailchimp is better for general email marketing and beginners.

Is Klaviyo cheaper than Mailchimp?

No, Klaviyo is typically more expensive than Mailchimp. Mailchimp offers a free plan up to 500 contacts, while Klaviyo's free plan is limited to 250 contacts. At 10,000 contacts, Klaviyo costs ~$150/month vs Mailchimp's ~$95/month.

Can I switch from Mailchimp to Klaviyo?

Yes, you can migrate from Mailchimp to Klaviyo. Klaviyo offers migration tools and support. You'll need to export your list, set up Klaviyo, and re-create your automations. Plan for 1-2 weeks for a full migration.

Does Mailchimp have abandoned cart?

Yes, but only on Standard plan ($20+/mo) and higher. Klaviyo includes abandoned cart on all plans including free, with more sophisticated flows.

Which has better deliverability?

Both have good deliverability (~98-99% inbox rate). Klaviyo has slightly better deliverability for e-commerce due to its specialized infrastructure, but the difference is minimal.
