Detailed Pricing Breakdown

Klaviyo Pricing

Klaviyo offers one of the best values for e-commerce due to its specialized features:

  • Free: 250 contacts, 500 email sends/month
  • 500 contacts: $20/month
  • 2,500 contacts: $60/month
  • 10,000 contacts: $150/month
  • 50,000 contacts: $550/month

SMS is charged separately at approximately $0.01-0.02 per message.

Mailchimp Pricing

Mailchimp has a generous free tier but costs increase quickly:

  • Free: 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month

  • 10,000 contacts: $95/month (Essentials), $115/month (Standard)

Important: E-commerce features require Standard plan ($20+).

What Affects Email Marketing Costs

Contact Count vs. Email Sends

Most tools (Klaviyo, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign) charge based on contact count. Some (Sendinblue) charge based on email volume. Consider which model fits your sending habits:

  • High frequency sender: Contact-based pricing is usually better
  • Low frequency sender: Send-based pricing may save money

Hidden Costs to Consider

  • SMS add-ons: $0.01-0.02 per text message
  • Dedicated IP: $50-100/month (for high volume senders)
  • Additional users: Some tools charge for team seats
  • Template customization: May require designer fees
  • Migration costs: Switching platforms takes time and effort

ROI Calculation

Email marketing typically generates $36-42 per $1 spent. For a $30/month plan:

  • Break-even: $30 in monthly email revenue
  • Typical return: $1,000-1,300 in monthly revenue
  • High performers: $3,000+ in monthly revenue

Pricing FAQs

How much does email marketing cost per month?

Email marketing costs $0-500+ per month depending on your contact count. Free plans offer 250-1,000 contacts. Paid plans start at $13-30/month for small lists and scale up. Most e-commerce stores pay $30-150/month for 2,500-10,000 contacts.

What is the cheapest email marketing for e-commerce?

Mailchimp offers the cheapest entry point with 500 free contacts. For paid plans, Sendinblue (Brevo) offers excellent value at $25/month. Klaviyo's free plan gives 250 contacts, which is good for starting stores.

Is email marketing worth the cost for e-commerce?

Yes, email marketing typically returns $36-42 for every $1 spent. For a $30/month investment, you only need to generate $30 in revenue to break even. Most stores see email driving 20-30% of total revenue.

When should I upgrade from a free plan?

Upgrade when you hit your free plan's contact limit, or when you need advanced features like abandoned cart automation, revenue tracking, or advanced segmentation. For most stores, this happens at 250-1,000 contacts.

Do email marketing tools offer discounts?

Yes, most tools offer 15-20% discount for annual billing. Non-profits may qualify for additional discounts. Some tools offer startup programs with extended free periods. Always ask for volume discounts if you have 50,000+ contacts.

Key Takeaways

When evaluating these tools, consider your specific needs:

  • Budget: Monthly costs and hidden fees
  • Integrations: Compatibility with your existing tools
  • Scalability: Ability to grow with your business
  • Support: Available help resources and response times
  • Security: Data protection and compliance features

Take advantage of free trials to test each tool thoroughly. The best choice depends on your unique workflow, team size, and growth plans.
