Klaviyo vs Brevo: Complete Email Marketing Comparison (2026)

Published: March 1, 2026 | Updated: March 18, 2026 | Tools Reviews Hub Team

📋 Quick Verdict

  • Choose Klaviyo if: You run an e-commerce store and need deep customer data integration
  • Choose Brevo if: You want affordable email and SMS marketing for any business
  • Starting price: Klaviyo $20/month; Brevo $0 (free plan)
  • E-commerce focus: Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce
  • Best value: Brevo for budget-conscious businesses

Overview: Klaviyo vs Brevo

Klaviyo and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue, rebranded in 2023) serve different segments of the email marketing market. Klaviyo, founded in 2012, built its entire platform around e-commerce customer data, becoming the go-to choice for Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento stores. Brevo, founded in 2007 in Paris, offers a broader suite of marketing tools including email, SMS, chat, and CRM at more accessible price points.

Klaviyo has become synonymous with e-commerce email marketing, offering deep integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, and other platforms that sync customer behavior, purchase history, and product data. Brevo positions itself as an all-in-one marketing platform for businesses of all sizes, with strong SMS marketing capabilities and a generous free tier.

The key difference: Klaviyo is purpose-built for e-commerce with deep customer data integration. Brevo is a broader marketing platform at a lower price point. Choose Klaviyo if you're an e-commerce store that can justify the cost; choose Brevo if you want affordable multi-channel marketing for any business type.

E-commerce Integration Comparison

Klaviyo E-commerce Features

  • Deep Shopify integration: Automatic sync of products, orders, customer data
  • WooCommerce integration: Real-time sync with WooCommerce stores
  • Product recommendations: AI-powered product blocks in emails
  • Customer lifetime value: Track LTV and segment by value
  • Abandoned cart: Sophisticated abandoned cart flows with product data
  • Browse abandonment: Track and message based on browsing behavior
  • Purchase behavior: Segment by purchase frequency, category, AOV
  • Predictive analytics: Expected next purchase date, churn risk

Brevo E-commerce Features

  • Shopify integration: Available through app marketplace
  • WooCommerce integration: Plugin available
  • Abandoned cart: Basic abandoned cart automation
  • Order tracking: Sync purchase data for segmentation
  • Product blocks: Include products in emails (limited vs Klaviyo)
  • CRM included: Built-in CRM for customer management

E-commerce verdict: Klaviyo is significantly better for e-commerce. Its deep integration with Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento provides customer data that Brevo simply can't match. If you run an e-commerce store and can afford Klaviyo, the ROI from better segmentation and automation usually justifies the cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Klaviyo for non-ecommerce?

Yes, but it's often overkill and expensive. Klaviyo works for any business, but its value proposition centers on e-commerce customer data. For content creators, B2B, or service businesses, Brevo offers better value. You're paying a premium for e-commerce features you won't use.

Does Brevo have abandoned cart automation?

Yes, Brevo offers abandoned cart automation for Shopify and WooCommerce stores. However, it's not as sophisticated as Klaviyo's. Klaviyo tracks browsing behavior, shows exact products viewed, and includes product images automatically. Brevo's is more basic.

Can I migrate from Klaviyo to Brevo?

Yes, you can export contacts from Klaviyo and import them to Brevo. You'll lose Klaviyo-specific data like customer lifetime value predictions and browsing behavior history. Automation sequences and templates will need to be recreated. For most businesses, migration is straightforward.

Which has better deliverability?

Both platforms have excellent deliverability (97%+). Klaviyo has strong reputation with e-commerce ISPs. Brevo has improved significantly since rebranding. Both authenticate properly and maintain good sending reputation. Neither has a clear advantage.

Does Klaviyo include SMS?

Yes, Klaviyo includes SMS as an add-on to any paid plan. You pay for SMS separately based on segments sent. Brevo also offers SMS, often with more international coverage. For US-focused e-commerce, Klaviyo's SMS integration is tighter. For international SMS, compare rates.

Final Verdict

🏆 Best for E-commerce: Klaviyo

Klaviyo wins for e-commerce stores that can justify the premium. The deep Shopify/Wooocommerce integration, customer data, product recommendations, and predictive analytics provide ROI that typically exceeds the cost for stores with meaningful email revenue. If you're running 5+ figures/month in email revenue, Klaviyo is worth it.

🏆 Best for Budget & Versatility: Brevo

Brevo wins for businesses that want affordable, multi-channel marketing. The included CRM, chat, and generous free tier make it excellent value. For non-ecommerce businesses, service providers, and budget-conscious marketers, Brevo delivers most features at a fraction of Klaviyo's cost.

Our recommendation: If you're an e-commerce store generating significant revenue from email marketing, choose Klaviyo. For any other business type or budget-conscious e-commerce stores, choose Brevo. Both offer free tiers—test each with your actual workflows before committing.
