What is Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)?

Brevo, formerly known as Sendinblue, is an all-in-one marketing platform that combines email marketing, SMS marketing, marketing automation, and CRM capabilities. For e-commerce businesses, it offers powerful automation workflows, transactional emails, and multi-channel marketing at competitive prices compared to dedicated e-commerce platforms like Klaviyo.

Founded in 2012 and rebranded to Brevo in 2023, the platform serves over 500,000 businesses worldwide. Its key differentiator is offering both email and SMS marketing under one roof with a generous free tier that supports up to 300 emails per day indefinitely.

Quick verdict: Brevo is ideal for e-commerce businesses that want affordable email + SMS marketing without sacrificing automation features. It's particularly strong for stores just starting out (free plan) and mid-size businesses wanting multi-channel marketing. For deeper Shopify integration, compare with Klaviyo.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brevo (Sendinblue) good for e-commerce?

Yes, Brevo is excellent for e-commerce businesses. It offers email and SMS marketing in one platform, integrations with Shopify and WooCommerce, abandoned cart automation, and competitive pricing. The free plan supports up to 300 emails/day, making it ideal for startups.

How much does Brevo cost for e-commerce?

Brevo offers a free plan for up to 300 emails/day. Paid plans start at $25/month (Starter) for 20,000 emails, $39/month (Business) for advanced automation, and custom pricing for Enterprise. SMS credits are purchased separately starting at $25 for 400 SMS.

Brevo vs Klaviyo for e-commerce: which is better?

Brevo is more affordable and offers both email and SMS in one platform. Klaviyo has deeper e-commerce integration and more advanced segmentation. For budget-conscious stores or those needing SMS, Brevo wins. For pure e-commerce analytics and segmentation, Klaviyo is stronger.

Can I use Brevo with Shopify?

Yes, Brevo has an official Shopify app available in the Shopify App Store. It syncs customer data, orders, and products automatically. Setup takes about 20 minutes and works on all Brevo plans including the free tier.

Does Brevo have abandoned cart automation?

Yes, Brevo has abandoned cart automation available on Business plans and higher. You can create multi-email sequences triggered when customers add items to cart but don't complete checkout. Average recovery rate is 8-12% of abandoned carts.

What happened to Sendinblue?

Sendinblue rebranded to Brevo in May 2023. The platform, features, and pricing remain the same—only the name changed. Your Sendinblue account automatically became a Brevo account with no action required.
