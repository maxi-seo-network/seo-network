FAQ

Is ChatGPT Plus worth it for realtors?

Yes, if you regularly create listing descriptions, emails, or marketing content. The $20/mo pays for itself with just 1-2 hours of time saved.

Can I use the free versions professionally?

Free tiers work for occasional use. For consistent professional use, paid plans offer better reliability and more features.

Which AI is best for listing descriptions?

ChatGPT Plus and Claude Pro both excel. ChatGPT has more templates; Claude produces more natural language.

How do I calculate ROI on AI tools?

Track your time on content tasks before and after AI. Multiply hours saved by your hourly rate. If you save 5 hours/month at $100/hour, that's $500 in value. A $20/mo tool pays for itself many times over.

Do I need multiple AI subscriptions?

Most agents need only one. ChatGPT Plus covers 90% of real estate tasks. Add Jasper only if you do heavy marketing content creation. Claude is good for long-form content but less versatile than ChatGPT.

What about AI image generation for listings?

ChatGPT Plus includes DALL-E for image generation. For professional listing photos, consider specialized tools like Canva Pro ($15/mo) or Adobe Creative Cloud ($55/mo) for advanced editing.

Are there real estate-specific AI tools?

Yes, tools like Real Estate AI, PropertyPen, and others focus on real estate. However, general AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude) are often better and cheaper. Use general AI first, then evaluate specialized tools.

How long does it take to see ROI?

Most agents see positive ROI within the first month. If you create 10+ listing descriptions monthly, AI pays for itself in the first week. Marketing teams see ROI even faster.
