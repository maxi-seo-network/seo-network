Why Claude for Realtors?

Claude by Anthropic stands out for its exceptional document analysis capabilities. With a 200,000 token context window (roughly 150,000 words), Claude can analyze entire contracts, inspection reports, and disclosure documents in a single conversation—something most other AI tools can't match.

For realtors who regularly work with lengthy legal documents, Claude offers superior comprehension and can provide detailed summaries, highlight key clauses, and answer specific questions about complex paperwork.

Key Features for Realtors

1. 200K Token Context Window

Claude can process approximately 150,000 words in a single conversation. This means you can upload entire purchase agreements, inspection reports, and HOA documents—and ask questions about any part of them.

2. Document Analysis

Upload PDFs, Word docs, or text files and Claude will:

  • Summarize key points
  • Highlight important clauses
  • Answer specific questions
  • Compare multiple documents

3. Projects

Create organized workspaces for different clients or transactions. Upload relevant documents and Claude will reference them in conversations.

4. Claude Artifacts

Generate documents, code, and formatted content that appear in a side panel. Useful for creating contracts, marketing materials, and checklists.

FAQ

Is Claude good for realtors?

Yes, Claude is excellent for realtors who work with long documents like contracts, inspection reports, and disclosure forms. Its 200K token context window allows it to analyze entire documents that ChatGPT can't handle.

Is Claude better than ChatGPT for real estate?

For document analysis, Claude is superior due to its longer context window. For general use including image generation and app integrations, ChatGPT is more versatile. Many realtors use both tools for different purposes.

Can Claude read PDFs?

Yes, Claude can read PDFs, Word documents, and text files. Upload your contracts or reports and ask questions about specific sections or request summaries.
