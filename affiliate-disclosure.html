Affiliate Disclosure

How we earn revenue while providing free content

Last Updated: March 2026
Effective Date: March 2026
FTC Disclosure: Tools Reviews Hub is a participant in affiliate programs. When you click links on our site and make purchases, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This helps support our website and allows us to continue providing free content.

What This Means for You

Our reviews and recommendations are based on genuine research, testing, and analysis. Affiliate partnerships do NOT influence our editorial content. We recommend products because we believe they offer value to our readers, not because we're paid to promote them.

If a product is poor quality, we'll say so—even if we have an affiliate relationship with them.

Affiliate Programs We Participate In

Tools Reviews Hub participates in the following affiliate programs:

Amazon Associates Program

Tools Reviews Hub is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. Product prices and availability are accurate as of the date/time indicated and are subject to change. Any price and availability information displayed on Amazon at the time of purchase will apply to the purchase of the product.

SaaS Affiliate Programs

We participate in affiliate programs for various software companies, including but not limited to:

  • Email marketing platforms (Klaviyo, Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign)
  • CRM software (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
  • Project management tools (Asana, Monday, ClickUp, Notion)
  • SEO tools (Semrush, Ahrefs, Moz)
  • Accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks)
  • Other software tools reviewed on our site

Commissions vary by program and are typically a percentage of the sale or a fixed amount per referral.

How Affiliate Links Work

When you click an affiliate link on our website:

  1. You are redirected to the product website through our affiliate tracking
  2. If you make a purchase (sometimes within a certain timeframe), we earn a commission
  3. The price you pay is the same as if you visited the site directly
  4. You do NOT pay extra for using our links

Our Commitment to Honest Reviews

We maintain editorial independence in all our reviews. Here's how:

  • Unpaid reviews: We do not accept payment for positive reviews
  • Honest ratings: We highlight both pros and cons of every product
  • Independent research: Our recommendations are based on testing and analysis
  • Transparency: We clearly identify affiliate links and partnerships

Non-Affiliate Content

Not all content on Tools Reviews Hub includes affiliate links. Some reviews and articles may not have any affiliate relationship. In those cases, we provide information purely for educational purposes.

No, using affiliate links does not cost you anything extra. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you visited the product website directly. The commission comes from the merchant's marketing budget, not from your purchase price.

Absolutely not. We maintain strict editorial independence in all our reviews. We do not accept payment for positive reviews. We highlight both pros and cons of every product, and if a product is poor quality, we'll say so—even if we have an affiliate relationship with them.

Yes. Our recommendations are based on genuine research, testing, and analysis. Affiliate partnerships do NOT influence our editorial content. We recommend products because we believe they offer value to our readers, not because we're paid to promote them.
