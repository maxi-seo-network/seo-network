Feature Comparison

Speed & Performance

Bluehost: Average speed (~800ms TTFB). Uses standard shared hosting infrastructure. Good for low-traffic sites, slower for high-traffic.

SiteGround: Fast (~400ms TTFB). Uses Google Cloud infrastructure with custom caching. Excellent for WordPress performance.

WP Engine: Fastest (~200ms TTFB). Premium infrastructure with built-in caching and CDN. Built specifically for WordPress performance.

Uptime & Reliability

Bluehost: ~99.96% uptime. Occasional outages reported during traffic spikes. Not the most reliable for business-critical sites.

SiteGround: ~99.99% uptime. Excellent reliability with proactive monitoring. 30-day money-back guarantee.

WP Engine: ~99.95% uptime. SLA available. 60-day money-back guarantee on annual plans.

Customer Support

Bluehost: 24/7 phone and chat. Average wait times (5-10 min). Knowledge base is helpful. Not WordPress-specific.

SiteGround: Legendary support. 24/7 chat with ~2 min wait times. WordPress experts. Best support in shared hosting.

WP Engine: Premium support. 24/7 chat/phone. WordPress-specific engineers. Fast response times.

Choose Bluehost If:

Choose SiteGround If:

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better: Bluehost or SiteGround?

SiteGround is better for performance and support. Bluehost is cheaper for beginners. Choose SiteGround for growing businesses, Bluehost for tight budgets.

Is WP Engine worth the price?

WP Engine is worth it for business-critical WordPress sites. If you're running a serious business and can't afford downtime or slow speeds, WP Engine's premium infrastructure and support justify the cost.

Can I switch hosts later?

Yes, all three hosts allow migration. SiteGround and WP Engine offer free migration. Bluehost may require manual migration. Plan 1-2 hours for migration.

Do I need managed WordPress hosting?

Managed WordPress hosting (like WP Engine) is worth it if: you have high traffic, run an e-commerce store, or don't want to manage technical details. For simple blogs, Bluehost or SiteGround are sufficient.
