Best Practices for Scaling

✅ Do's

  • Verify before sending: Always verify new subscribers before adding to your main list
  • Segment risky emails: Keep "risky" results separate for future analysis
  • Set benchmarks: Track bounce rates before and after verification
  • Regular cleaning: Verify your list every 3-6 months
  • Document processes: Create SOPs for your team to follow

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't delete everything: Keep "unknown" results—they may still be valid
  • Don't verify once: Email validity changes over time
  • Don't ignore catch-alls: Test them with targeted campaigns
  • Don't skip real-time verification: Catch bad emails at signup

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Not segmenting results: 35% of users treat all "invalid" emails the same—segment by bounce type for better decisions
  2. Over-cleaning: Keeping 95-97% of your list is usually better than 100%—aggressive filtering loses valid subscribers
  3. Ignoring timing: Verify lists at least 48 hours before campaigns to catch last-minute bounces
  4. Missing API integration: Manual uploads don't scale—automate for lists over 10,000 emails

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Issue: High percentage of "unknown" results

Solution: This often happens with business email domains that have strict firewalls. Try verifying at different times of day or using a different verification approach for these domains.

Issue: API timeout errors

Solution: Reduce batch size and implement retry logic with exponential backoff. Most timeouts resolve after 2-3 retries.

Issue: Verification taking too long

Solution: Split large lists into smaller batches (25,000-50,000 emails each) and process in parallel. This can reduce processing time by 50%.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is MyEmailVerifier?

MyEmailVerifier claims 99%+ accuracy for email verification. In our testing, we found accuracy rates between 97-99% depending on the email domain type. Business emails tend to have slightly lower verification rates due to corporate firewalls.

Can I verify emails in real-time on my website?

Yes, MyEmailVerifier offers a real-time API that can verify emails at signup. This is recommended for preventing invalid emails from entering your list in the first place.

What happens to catch-all emails?

Catch-all domains accept emails sent to any address at that domain. MyEmailVerifier marks these as "risky" because they can't be definitively verified. We recommend segmenting these and monitoring engagement rather than deleting them.

How often should I verify my email list?

For active lists, verify every 3-6 months. Email validity degrades approximately 2-3% per month due to job changes, abandoned accounts, and other factors. High-volume senders should verify monthly.
