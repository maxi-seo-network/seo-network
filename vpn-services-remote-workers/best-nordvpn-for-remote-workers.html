Why Remote Workers Need a VPN

Working remotely means relying on internet connections you don't control. Coffee shops, airports, hotels, and coworking spaces all present security risks. A VPN encrypts your traffic, protecting:

  • Work documents: Sensitive client data, internal communications
  • Login credentials: Passwords, two-factor codes, session tokens
  • Financial information: Banking, invoicing, payment processing
  • Personal browsing: Email, social media, messaging apps

NordVPN has become the VPN of choice for remote workers because it balances speed, security, and affordability better than competitors.

NordVPN for Remote Work: Use Cases

Working from Coffee Shops

Coffee shop WiFi is notoriously insecure. NordVPN protects your work from:

  • Packet sniffing (hackers capturing your data)
  • Man-in-the-middle attacks
  • Rogue hotspots (fake WiFi networks)

Setup: Enable Threat Protection, turn on Kill Switch, connect before opening work apps.

Digital Nomad Travel

Working while traveling internationally? NordVPN helps you:

  • Access geo-restricted work tools (some countries block services)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NordVPN good for remote work?

Yes, NordVPN is excellent for remote work. With 6,100+ servers in 111 countries, a strict no-logs policy, and fast speeds (average 475 Mbps on WireGuard), it's ideal for remote workers who need secure connections to company resources, access to geo-restricted content, and privacy on public WiFi.

How much does NordVPN cost for remote workers?

NordVPN costs $3.09-12.99/month depending on plan length. The 2-year plan offers the best value at $3.09/month (billed $79 every 2 years). Remote workers should consider the Plus or Ultimate plans for threat protection and password manager features.

Can employers see my activity with NordVPN?

NordVPN encrypts your traffic, so ISPs and public WiFi operators cannot see your activity. However, if you're connected to a company VPN or corporate network, your employer may still monitor activity on their network. NordVPN protects you from outside observers, not from your employer's internal monitoring.

Which NordVPN plan is best for remote workers?

For most remote workers, NordVPN Plus ($4.09/month on 2-year plan) offers the best value. It includes threat protection (malware blocking, ad blocking) and password manager. Ultimate adds cloud storage and data breach monitoring for security-conscious workers.

Does NordVPN work with company VPN?

You can run NordVPN and a company VPN simultaneously, but it may cause connection issues. Best practice is to connect to your company VPN for work tasks and use NordVPN for personal browsing on public WiFi. NordVPN split tunneling allows you to route only specific apps through the VPN.
