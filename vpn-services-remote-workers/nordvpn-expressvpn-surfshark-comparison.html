Feature Comparison for Remote Work

Speed & Performance

NordVPN: Very fast with NordLynx protocol. Typically 400-600 Mbps on nearby servers. Great for video calls and large file transfers.

ExpressVPN: Consistently fast with Lightway protocol. 300-500 Mbps typical. Most reliable across all locations.

Surfshark: Good speeds with WireGuard. 200-400 Mbps typical. Slower on distant servers but still sufficient for work.

Security & Privacy

NordVPN: Military-grade AES-256 encryption, independent audits, Panama jurisdiction. Double VPN option for extra security.

ExpressVPN: AES-256 encryption, TrustedServer technology (RAM-only), British Virgin Islands jurisdiction. Excellent privacy track record.

Surfshark: AES-256-GCM encryption, independently audited, Netherlands jurisdiction. CleanWeb ad blocker included.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better: NordVPN or ExpressVPN?

NordVPN is better for value and features (6,000+ servers, specialty servers). ExpressVPN is better for simplicity and consistent speeds. Choose NordVPN for features, ExpressVPN for ease of use.

Is Surfshark as good as NordVPN?

Surfshark offers similar features to NordVPN at a lower price. NordVPN has more servers and better brand reputation. Surfshark allows unlimited devices. For most users, they're comparable in quality.

Which VPN is best for remote work?

For remote work, NordVPN or Surfshark offer the best value. ExpressVPN is best if budget isn't a concern. All three work well for accessing company resources securely, video calls, and protecting data on public WiFi.

Can I use these VPNs for Netflix?

Yes, all three (NordVPN, ExpressVPN, Surfshark) reliably unblock Netflix US and other regions. ExpressVPN is most consistent, but all three work well.

Which VPN is best for teams?

Surfshark is best for teams on a budget (unlimited devices). NordVPN Teams offers centralized management. ExpressVPN has business plans but is most expensive.
