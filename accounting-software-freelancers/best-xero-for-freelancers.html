Best Xero for Freelancers in 2026: Pricing, Plans & Complete Guide

Everything freelancers need to know about Xero plans, pricing, and features

Quick Comparison

Best for Low-Volume Freelancers: Xero Starter at $15/month (20 invoices)

Best Value Overall: Xero Standard at $30/month (unlimited invoicing)

Best for Multi-Currency: Xero Premium at $42/month

Free Trial: 30 days, no credit card required

Why Xero Stands Out for Freelancers

Xero has earned a reputation as the accountant's favorite cloud accounting software—and for good reason. While QuickBooks dominates the US market, Xero has become the go-to choice for freelancers who want seamless collaboration with their CPAs and bookkeepers.

The platform excels at bank reconciliation, handles multi-currency transactions beautifully, and offers an intuitive dashboard that gives you a clear picture of your business finances at a glance. For freelancers who work with international clients or value accountant collaboration, Xero deserves serious consideration.

Xero Starter: Budget-Friendly Entry Point

What's Included

  • 20 Invoices per month: Create and send professional invoices
  • 5 Bills per month: Track what you owe vendors
  • 20 Bank Transaction Lines: Automatically import and categorize
  • Bank Reconciliation: Match transactions to your bank statements
  • Receipt Capture: Snap photos of receipts
  • Financial Reporting: Balance sheet, P&L, cash flow
  • Mobile App: Full-featured iOS and Android
  • Accountant Access: Invite your CPA or bookkeeper

Critical Limitations

⚠️ Starter Plan Restrictions:
  • 20 invoice limit - Quickly restrictive for active freelancers
  • 5 bill limit - Not suitable if you have many vendors
  • 20 bank transaction lines - Includes all transactions, not just business
  • No multi-currency - International clients will need Premium

When Starter Works

The Starter plan is viable if you:

  • Have fewer than 20 invoices per month (fewer than 20 clients)
  • Work primarily with domestic clients
  • Have simple bookkeeping needs
  • Want the lowest possible cost

Xero Standard: The Sweet Spot for Most Freelancers

What's Included (Everything in Starter, plus)

  • Unlimited Invoices: Send as many invoices as needed
  • Unlimited Bills: Track all vendor payments
  • Unlimited Bank Transactions: No caps on reconciliation
  • Multi-Currency: Invoice in USD, EUR, GBP, and 160+ currencies
  • Short-term Cash Flow: See your cash position 30 days out
  • Batch Payments: Pay multiple bills at once

Why Standard Is Worth Double the Price

At $30/month, Xero Standard costs twice as much as Starter—but it eliminates all the limits that make Starter frustrating:

  • Unlimited invoicing: Never worry about hitting a cap
  • Multi-currency: Work with international clients seamlessly
  • Full bank reconciliation: No transaction limits
💡 Our Recommendation: Most freelancers should choose Standard. The 20-invoice limit on Starter is too restrictive for growing businesses, and multi-currency is essential if you ever work internationally.

Xero Premium: For International Freelancers

What's Included (Everything in Standard, plus)

  • Multi-Currency Advanced: Real-time exchange rates and accounting
  • Unlimited Users: Add team members at no extra cost
  • Project Tracking: Detailed profitability by project
  • Expense Claims: Advanced expense management

When Premium Makes Sense

Consider Premium ($42/month) if you:

  • Regularly invoice in multiple currencies
  • Need project-level profitability tracking
  • Have multiple team members accessing the account
  • Want advanced analytics and reporting

When to Choose Xero

Choose Xero if you:

  • Work with an accountant who prefers Xero
  • Invoice international clients in multiple currencies
  • Want the best bank reconciliation experience
  • Prefer a modern, intuitive interface
  • Need extensive third-party integrations
  • Want unlimited users (Premium plan)

Consider alternatives if you:

  • Need built-in tax estimation (QuickBooks Self-Employed)
  • Want the best invoicing experience (FreshBooks)
  • Need mileage tracking (QuickBooks or FreshBooks)
  • Want a free plan (Wave)
  • Invoice fewer than 20 clients and want lowest cost (Xero Starter works)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Xero good for freelancers?

Yes, Xero is excellent for freelancers, especially those who work with accountants. Xero's strongest advantage is its collaboration features—your accountant can access your books directly, making tax time much smoother. The interface is modern and the mobile app is well-designed.

Which Xero plan is best for freelancers?

For most freelancers, Xero Starter at $15/month is sufficient if you have minimal invoicing needs. However, if you send more than 20 invoices per month or need multi-currency support, Xero Standard at $30/month offers better value with unlimited invoicing.

Does Xero have a free plan?

No, Xero does not offer a free plan. However, they provide a free trial (typically 30 days) to test the platform. For free accounting software, Wave Accounting is a good alternative, though Xero offers more advanced features.

Can I track time in Xero?

Yes, Xero includes built-in time tracking that integrates with projects and invoices. You can log hours manually or use the timer, then convert tracked time directly into invoice line items. This is particularly useful for freelancers who bill hourly.

What's the difference between Xero Starter and Standard?

Xero Starter ($15/month) limits you to 20 invoices and 5 bills per month, plus 20 bank transaction lines. Xero Standard ($30/month) removes all invoice/bill limits and includes multi-currency support. If you invoice more than 20 clients monthly or work with international clients, Standard is necessary.

Final Verdict

🎯 Recommendations by Freelancer Type

Fewer than 20 invoices/month: Start with Xero Starter at $15/month. You can upgrade anytime.

More than 20 invoices/month: Choose Xero Standard at $30/month for unlimited invoicing.

International freelancers: Choose Xero Premium at $42/month for advanced multi-currency.

Working with an accountant: Any Xero plan works well—accountant access is included on all tiers.

Bottom line: Xero is the best choice for freelancers who work with accountants or international clients. The collaboration features are unmatched, and multi-currency support is essential for global freelancers. Just be aware that you'll need the Standard plan or higher for most practical use—the Starter limits are too restrictive.
