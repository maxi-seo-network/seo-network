If your Ledger device is new, you'll need to set it up first:

Security Best Practices

Using Ledger Wallet with a hardware wallet provides strong security, but following best practices is essential to maximize protection of your cryptocurrency assets.

Recovery Phrase Security

  • Never store your recovery phrase digitally – no photos, cloud storage, emails, or password managers
  • Use metal backup solutions for fire and water resistance
  • Store multiple copies in different secure physical locations
  • Never share your recovery phrase with anyone, including Ledger support

Device Security

  • Always verify transactions on your Ledger device screen – never blindly approve on your computer
  • Keep your device firmware updated through official Ledger Wallet updates
  • Use a strong, unique PIN code for your Ledger device
  • Enable passphrase feature for an additional layer of security (advanced users)

Computer and Network Security

  • Use a clean, dedicated computer for cryptocurrency transactions if possible
  • Keep your operating system and antivirus updated
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi when managing cryptocurrency
  • Bookmark official Ledger URLs and always verify you're on the legitimate site
  • Be wary of phishing attempts via email, social media, or fake websites

Transaction Verification

  • Always verify recipient addresses on your Ledger device screen, not just on your computer
  • Check transaction amounts on the device before signing
  • Start with small test transactions when sending significant amounts

Common Issues and Solutions

Even with careful setup, users occasionally encounter issues. Here are common problems and their solutions:

Device Not Recognized

Problem: Ledger Wallet doesn't detect your connected device.

Solutions:

  • Try a different USB cable (some cables are charge-only)
  • Try a different USB port, preferably directly on the computer
  • Close other applications that might access the device
  • Restart your Ledger device and Ledger Wallet application
  • Check if your device needs a firmware update

App Installation Fails

Problem: Unable to install crypto apps on your Ledger device.

Solutions:

  • Ensure your device has sufficient storage (Nano S has limited space)
  • Update your device firmware to the latest version
  • Uninstall unused apps to free up space
  • Check your internet connection

Transaction Pending or Stuck

Problem: A transaction is stuck in pending status.

Solutions:

  • Wait for network congestion to clear
  • Use the "Replace by Fee" feature if available to increase gas/fees
  • Check blockchain explorers for transaction status

Balance Not Showing Correctly

Problem: Portfolio shows incorrect or outdated balances.

Solutions:

  • Refresh your accounts in Ledger Wallet
  • Check if you're looking at the correct network/chain
  • Clear the app cache and resync
  • Verify transaction history on a blockchain explorer
