Step 2: Complete Verification (KYC)

Required Documents

  • Passport or National ID
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Selfie verification

Process

  1. Go to Account → Verification
  2. Upload ID document
  3. Take selfie
  4. Upload proof of address
  5. Wait for approval (minutes to days)

Step 3: Fund Your Account

European Payment Methods

SEPA Transfer Steps

  1. Go to Deposit → Fiat → EUR
  2. Select SEPA
  3. Copy bank details
  4. Transfer from your bank
  5. Wait 1-3 business days

Step 4: Understand Market Types

Spot Trading

Buy and sell actual cryptocurrencies. Best for beginners.

Futures Trading

Trade contracts with leverage. Higher risk. Not recommended for beginners.

Step 5: Learn Basic Analysis

Tools Needed

  • TradingView: Free charts and indicators
  • CoinGecko: Price tracking
  • Exchange charts: Built-in analysis

Basic Indicators

  • Price Action: Support and resistance levels
  • Moving Averages: Trend direction
  • RSI: Overbought/oversold conditions
  • Volume: Confirms trend strength

Step 6: Risk Management

Golden Rules

🛡️ Essential Rules:
  • Risk max 1-2% per trade
  • Always use stop-loss
  • Never average down without plan
  • No leverage as beginner
  • Keep trading journal

Position Sizing Example

Capital: €100

  • Risk per trade: €2 (2%)
  • Stop-loss: 5% below entry
  • Position size: €40 (40% max)

Step 7: Make Your First Trade

Step by Step

  1. Go to Trading → Spot
  2. Select pair (e.g., BTC/EUR)
  3. Choose order type (Limit recommended)
  4. Set price and amount
  5. Set stop-loss
  6. Confirm order

Order Types Explained

  • Market: Execute immediately at current price
  • Limit: Execute at specified price
  • Stop-Limit: Triggers when price reaches level

Common Beginner Mistakes

What NOT to Do

  • ❌ Use high leverage (10x, 20x, 100x)
  • ❌ Skip stop-loss
  • ❌ Trade with money you can't lose
  • ❌ Follow random "signals"
  • ❌ Panic sell during dips
  • ❌ Leave large amounts on exchanges
