Setup Guide

  1. Buy from Official Store
    Get Ledger Nano X from Ledger.com - Never buy from Amazon, eBay, or third parties.
  2. Initialize Device
    Turn on and choose "Set up as new device" or restore from recovery phrase.
  3. Set PIN
    Choose a PIN (4-8 digits). This protects your device if lost or stolen.
  4. Write Recovery Phrase
    Write down all 24 words in order. Never store digitally or take a photo.
  5. Install Ledger Live
    Download Ledger Live on desktop or mobile. Connect your device.
  6. Install Coin Apps
    Install apps for the cryptocurrencies you want to use (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ledger Nano X safe?

Yes, Ledger Nano X has never been hacked. The secure element (CC EAL5+ certified) keeps your private keys isolated. Always buy from the official Ledger store.

Can I use Ledger Nano X with iPhone?

Yes, Ledger Nano X works with iPhone via Bluetooth. It's the only hardware wallet that works with iOS. Download Ledger Live from the App Store.

What happens if I lose my Ledger Nano X?

Your crypto is safe. Use your 24-word recovery phrase to restore your wallet on any Ledger device. Never share your recovery phrase.

Does Ledger Nano X support NFTs?

Yes, Ledger Nano X supports Ethereum and Solana NFTs. View them in Ledger Live or compatible third-party apps.

How long does the battery last?

The Ledger Nano X battery lasts 8+ hours of active use. In standby, it can last weeks. The battery is not user-replaceable.
