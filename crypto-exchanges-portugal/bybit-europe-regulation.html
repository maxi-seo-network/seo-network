What is MiCA and How Does It Affect Bybit?

MiCA is the European Union's comprehensive cryptocurrency regulation framework that came into full effect in December 2024. It establishes:

  • Authorization requirements: All crypto exchanges must obtain EU licenses
  • Consumer protections: Mandatory risk disclosures and complaint procedures
  • Capital requirements: Minimum reserves to ensure platform solvency
  • Anti-money laundering: Enhanced KYC and transaction monitoring
  • Stablecoin rules: Strict requirements for stablecoin issuers
