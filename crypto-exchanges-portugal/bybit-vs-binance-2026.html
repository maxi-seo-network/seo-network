Overview of Both Exchanges

Binance, founded in 2017 by Changpeng Zhao, is the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume. It offers over 350 cryptocurrencies and serves millions of users worldwide. The platform provides spot trading, futures, options, staking, and various DeFi services.

Sign up for Binance to access one of the most comprehensive crypto ecosystems available.

Bybit

Bybit, launched in 2018, has grown rapidly to become a top derivatives exchange. It focuses on perpetual contracts and offers up to 100x leverage.
