What is MiCA Regulation?

MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets) is the European Union's comprehensive regulatory framework for cryptocurrencies, which came into full effect in December 2024. It represents the world's first comprehensive crypto regulation and sets clear standards for:

  • Crypto-asset service providers (CASPs) — exchanges, wallets, and custodians
  • Stablecoin issuers — requirements for reserves and redemption rights
  • Consumer protection — transparency, complaint handling, and safeguards
  • Market integrity — prevention of market abuse and manipulation
