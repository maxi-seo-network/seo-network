Hidden Fees to Watch Out For

How to Reduce Your Trading Fees

1. Use Limit Orders (Maker Orders)

Limit orders typically have lower fees than market orders. Instead of buying immediately at market price, set a limit order slightly above the current price.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are crypto trading fees tax-deductible in Europe?

Tax rules vary by country. In Portugal, crypto gains are generally tax-free for individuals. In Germany, holding for over 1 year makes gains tax-free. Consult a local tax professional for specific advice.

Do fees differ between Bitcoin and altcoins?

Generally no, most exchanges charge the same percentage regardless of the cryptocurrency. However, withdrawal fees for smaller coins may be higher due to network costs.

Is it cheaper to buy crypto with EUR or stablecoins?

Buying stablecoins (USDT/USDC) with EUR first, then trading for other crypto, can sometimes be cheaper than direct EUR-to-crypto purchases, depending on the exchange's fee structure.

Do VIP tiers matter for casual traders?

For most retail traders, VIP tiers are not achievable. Focus on using BNB discounts (Binance) and limit orders instead. VIP tiers typically require $50,000+ monthly volume.
