Understanding the Risks

Before using any crypto lending platform, it's essential to understand the risks involved:

Platform Risk

When you deposit crypto on Nexo, you're trusting them to manage it safely. While Nexo has strong security and insurance, no platform is risk-free. Events like the FTX collapse in 2022 highlighted the potential for platform failures in the crypto industry.

Market Risk

Cryptocurrency prices are highly volatile. The value of your holdings can decrease significantly, even while earning interest. This is particularly relevant for collateral used in credit lines—if your collateral value drops, you may face a margin call.

Interest Rate Risk

Nexo's interest rates are not guaranteed and can change at any time. Rates depend on platform performance, market conditions, and Nexo's policies. Don't make financial decisions based on rates remaining constant.

Regulatory Risk

Crypto regulations are evolving globally and within the EU. Changes in regulations could affect Nexo's ability to operate in certain jurisdictions or offer specific products.

Smart Contract and Technical Risk

While Nexo doesn't rely heavily on smart contracts (unlike DeFi platforms), technical issues, hacks, or vulnerabilities could potentially affect the platform.

⚠️ Risk Management Recommendations:
