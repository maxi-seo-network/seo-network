1. Exchange Built-in Charts

Most exchanges offer free charts:

  • Binance: TradingView-powered charts
  • Kraken: Advanced charting
  • Coinbase: Basic charts

Pros

  • Free
  • Real-time data
  • Trade directly from charts

Cons

  • Can't compare exchanges
  • Limited customization
  • Only that exchange's pairs

2. Bitbo

Free Bitcoin-focused charting:

  • Real-time Bitcoin charts
  • Technical indicators
  • On-chain data
  • Completely free

Best For

Bitcoin traders who don't need altcoins.

3. CoinGecko/CoinMarketCap

Free basic charts:

  • Price history
  • Volume charts
  • Basic indicators
  • Portfolio tracking

Limitations

  • Not for advanced technical analysis
  • Limited indicators
  • Delayed data sometimes

4. Yahoo Finance

Free stock and crypto charts:

  • Good for stocks
  • Basic crypto charts
  • News integration
  • Portfolio tracking

Limitations

  • Delayed data on free plan
  • Limited crypto pairs

5. TradingView Free (Optimized)
