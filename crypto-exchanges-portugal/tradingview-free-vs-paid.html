Honest Free Plan Limitations for Crypto Traders

While the free plan provides genuine value, crypto traders face specific challenges:

  1. Only 2 indicators per chart — Most crypto technical analysis requires combining at least 3-5 indicators (RSI + MACD + Volume + Moving Averages). Two indicators severely limits analysis depth.
  2. 3 price alerts maximum — Cryptocurrency markets operate 24/7 with extreme volatility. Three alerts get consumed quickly monitoring Bitcoin, Ethereum, and one altcoin.
  3. Single chart layout — Cannot compare Bitcoin price action with altcoin movements simultaneously without manually switching.
  4. No second-based intervals — Scalping strategies using 15-second or 30-second candles are impossible on the free plan.
  5. Ads displayed — Banner ads consume screen space, distracting from analysis.
  6. 30-symbol watchlist limit — Active crypto traders often monitor 50-100+ coins across exchanges.

Verdict: The free plan works perfectly for casual investors checking Bitcoin prices weekly, learning technical analysis basics, or following 1-2 cryptocurrencies. Active traders will outgrow it within days.
