Step-by-Step Guide for Binance

Step 1: Create an Account

  1. Go to Binance.com
  2. Click "Register" and enter your email
  3. Create a strong password
  4. Verify your email address

Step 2: Complete KYC Verification

  1. Go to Account → Identification
  2. Upload your ID or passport
  3. Take a selfie for face verification
  4. Wait for approval (usually 10-30 minutes)

Step 3: Deposit Euros

Via SEPA (Recommended - Usually Free):

  1. Go to Wallet → Fiat and Spot
  2. Click "Deposit" next to EUR
  3. Select "Bank Transfer (SEPA)"
  4. Copy Binance's bank details
  5. Transfer from your bank (usually arrives same-day or next day)

Via Credit/Debit Card (Instant):

  1. Go to Wallet → Fiat and Spot
  2. Click "Deposit" next to EUR
  3. Select "Card"
  4. Enter card details and amount
  5. Complete 3D Secure verification

Step 4: Buy Bitcoin

  1. Go to Trade → Convert
  2. Select EUR → BTC
  3. Enter the amount
  4. Click "Convert"
  5. Bitcoin appears in your wallet instantly

Fees for Instant Bitcoin Purchases

Trading Fees

  • Binance: 0.1% (can be reduced with BNB)
  • Bybit: 0.1%
  • Kraken: 0.16% maker / 0.26% taker

Deposit Fees

  • SEPA: Usually free on most exchanges
  • Card: 1.5-3.9% typical

Spread

Card purchases often include a spread markup. You may pay slightly above market price. For best rates, deposit via SEPA and use spot trading.

Security Tips for First-Time Buyers

  1. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) - Use Google Authenticator or similar, not SMS
  2. Use a Hardware Wallet - For amounts over €500, consider a Trezor or Ledger
  3. Whitelist Withdrawal Addresses - Prevent unauthorized transfers
  4. Never Share Your Seed Phrase - No legitimate service will ask for it
  5. Bookmark Official Sites - Avoid phishing by bookmarking official exchange URLs

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not verifying before depositing: Complete KYC first to avoid frozen funds
  • Sending to wrong network: Double-check the network (BTC vs BEP20 vs ERC20)
  • Using weak passwords: Use a password manager like NordPass
  • Leaving funds on exchange: Not your keys, not your coins
  • Ignoring fees: Small purchases get eaten by percentage fees

FAQ

How fast can I buy Bitcoin with Euro?

Instant purchases with card take 5-10 minutes. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days. SEPA transfers in Europe are often same-day.

What's the cheapest way to buy Bitcoin with Euro?

Bank transfer/SEPA deposits usually have lowest fees (often free). Card purchases have higher fees (1.5-3.9%). Always compare fees on official exchange websites.

Is it safe to buy Bitcoin with my credit card?

Yes, on reputable exchanges like Binance. Use two-factor authentication and a hardware wallet for maximum security. Never share your card details on unverified platforms.

Can I buy Bitcoin without verification?

Most regulated exchanges require KYC for EUR purchases. P2P platforms may offer alternatives with limits. Always comply with local regulations.

What's the minimum Bitcoin purchase?

Most exchanges allow purchases as low as €10-15 worth of Bitcoin. You don't need to buy a whole Bitcoin.
