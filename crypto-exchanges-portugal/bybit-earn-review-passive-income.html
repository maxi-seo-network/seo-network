Bybit Earn Products Overview

Bybit Earn is organized into several product categories, each catering to different investor needs:

1. Easy Earn (Flexible Savings)

Easy Earn is Bybit's simplest passive income product, functioning like a crypto savings account:

  • How it works: Deposit supported cryptocurrencies and earn daily interest
  • Withdrawal: Instant anytime withdrawal with no penalties
  • Minimum deposit: Often as low as $1 equivalent
  • Typical APY: 1-10% depending on the asset
  • Risk level: Low (but still subject to crypto market volatility)

Easy Earn is ideal for beginners who want liquidity while earning yield. You can withdraw your funds at any time without losing accumulated interest, making it perfect for parking funds you might need to trade.

2. On-Chain Earn (Staking)

On-Chain Earn involves staking proof-of-stake cryptocurrencies directly on the blockchain through Bybit:

  • Flexible Staking: Similar to Easy Earn but for PoS tokens like ETH, SOL, ADA
  • Locked Staking: Higher APY for committing funds for 30-90 days
  • Typical APY: 2-20% depending on the cryptocurrency
  • Popular coins: ETH, SOL, MATIC, ADA, DOT, and more

Key advantage: Bybit handles the technical complexity of staking - you simply deposit and earn. This is much easier than setting up your own staking node or navigating DeFi protocols.

3. Liquidity Mining

For more advanced users, Liquidity Mining offers potentially higher returns:

  • How it works: Provide liquidity to trading pairs on Bybit's decentralized exchange integration
  • Rewards: Trading fees + additional token incentives
  • Risk: Impermanent loss (you may receive fewer tokens than deposited if prices diverge)
  • APY range: Can vary significantly from 5% to 50%+ depending on pool

Important: Liquidity mining carries higher risk. Only use this product if you understand impermanent loss and are comfortable with the possibility of receiving a different asset than you deposited.

4. Dual Asset

Dual Asset is an advanced product that can yield high returns but requires understanding:

  • How it works: You deposit one cryptocurrency and agree to potentially receive a different one
  • Example: Deposit BTC, potentially receive USDT if price reaches a certain level
  • APY range: 10-35%+ but highly variable
  • Risk: Settlement risk, market volatility exposure

Not recommended for beginners. This product has significant complexity and you should fully understand the settlement mechanism before using it.

    Risks and Considerations

    While Bybit Earn offers attractive yields, all crypto investments carry risk. Understanding these risks is essential:

    Market Risk

    The value of your cryptocurrency can decrease significantly. A 10% APY on an asset that drops 30% in value still results in a net loss. Consider whether earning interest makes sense if you believe the asset will decline.

    Platform Risk

    Centralized exchanges like Bybit hold custody of your funds. While Bybit employs security measures, exchange hacks, insolvencies, or regulatory actions could impact your assets. The adage "not your keys, not your crypto" applies.

    Smart Contract Risk (DeFi Products)

    On-Chain and DeFi products involve smart contracts that could have vulnerabilities. While Bybit vets these contracts, bugs can exist.

    Liquidity Risk

    Locked savings and staking products tie up your funds. You cannot quickly react to market changes or emergencies without penalties.

    Impermanent Loss (Liquidity Mining)

    If you provide liquidity to trading pairs, you may experience impermanent loss - receiving fewer tokens than you deposited if prices diverge significantly.

    Settlement Risk (Dual Asset)

    Dual Asset products involve settlement in potentially unwanted cryptocurrencies. Understand the settlement mechanics fully before using these products.
