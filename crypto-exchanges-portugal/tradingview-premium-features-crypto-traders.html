Advanced Chart Types for Crypto Analysis

TradingView supports various chart types beyond standard candlesticks. For cryptocurrency traders, certain advanced chart types provide significant analytical advantages:

Available on Plus and Higher:

  • Second-based intervals: Essential for scalpers and high-frequency crypto trading. 1-second, 5-second, 10-second charts reveal micro-movements invisible on minute charts.
  • Tick charts: Available on Premium and Ultimate. Plot every individual trade—critical for analyzing order flow and market microstructure on high-volume pairs.
  • Range bars: Available on Plus and higher. Each bar represents a fixed price movement regardless of time. Excellent for volatile crypto markets where price movement matters more than time.
  • Renko, Kagi, Line Break, Point & Figure: Available on Plus and higher. These noise-filtering chart types help crypto traders identify genuine trend reversals in highly volatile markets.

Recommendation: Scalpers and day traders absolutely need Plus for second-based intervals. Swing traders can work with Essential. Position traders may find the free plan sufficient.
