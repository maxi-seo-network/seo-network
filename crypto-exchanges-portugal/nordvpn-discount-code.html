Why Crypto Traders Need a VPN

Understanding the connection between VPNs and cryptocurrency security helps clarify why investing in a quality VPN service like NordVPN is worthwhile for traders in Portugal and Europe.

Protection on Public Networks

Crypto traders often work from various locations—cafés, coworking spaces, or while traveling. Public Wi-Fi networks in Portuguese cafés or European airports present security risks. A VPN encrypts your internet traffic, making it significantly harder for malicious actors to intercept your login credentials, API keys, or transaction data.

Bypassing Geographic Restrictions

Some cryptocurrency exchanges implement geographic restrictions. Certain features, trading pairs, or entire platforms may be unavailable in specific regions. A VPN allows you to connect through servers in different countries, potentially accessing services that would otherwise be restricted in your location.

Preventing ISP Monitoring

Internet Service Providers can technically monitor your online activities. While they cannot see the content of your encrypted connections to exchanges, they can see which exchanges you visit and when. A VPN adds an additional privacy layer by routing your traffic through encrypted tunnels.

Protection Against Phishing and DNS Attacks

NordVPN includes features like Threat Protection, which can help block malicious websites, ads, and trackers. This additional security layer can help protect crypto traders from accidentally visiting phishing sites designed to steal wallet credentials or exchange login information.
