How to Create a Kraken Account in Portugal

  1. Visit Kraken.com
    Go to the official Kraken website and click "Create Account".
  2. Enter Your Email
    Use a secure email address and create a strong password.
  3. Verify Your Identity
    Kraken requires KYC verification. Submit your ID (passport or national ID) and proof of address.
  4. Enable 2FA
    Set up two-factor authentication using an authenticator app (recommended) or SMS.
  5. Deposit EUR via SEPA
    Link your Portuguese bank account and deposit EUR via SEPA (free, 1-3 days).
  6. Start Trading
    Buy crypto with EUR or trade between cryptocurrencies.
