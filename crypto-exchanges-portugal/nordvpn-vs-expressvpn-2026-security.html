Security Features: Protecting Your Crypto Transactions

Encryption Standards

Both NordVPN and ExpressVPN use AES-256 encryption—the same standard employed by governments and security agencies worldwide. This level of encryption is virtually unbreakable with current technology, ensuring your crypto exchange logins and wallet transactions remain private.

For crypto traders specifically, both services offer:

  • Perfect Forward Secrecy (PFS) — New encryption keys for each session, meaning past sessions cannot be decrypted even if future keys are compromised
  • RSA-4096 key exchange — Secure handshake preventing man-in-the-middle attacks
  • SHA-512 authentication — Verifies server identity to prevent spoofing

NordVPN offers a unique Double VPN feature that routes your traffic through two separate VPN servers, encrypting your data twice. For crypto traders handling large transactions, this provides an extra layer of security—particularly useful when:
