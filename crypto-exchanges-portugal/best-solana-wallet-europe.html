Setting Up Phantom

  1. Download Phantom extension or app
  2. Create new wallet
  3. Write down seed phrase (paper only)
  4. Store seed phrase safely
  5. Transfer SOL to wallet
⚠️ Security: Never share your seed phrase. Write it on paper and store securely.

Staking SOL

In Phantom

  1. Open Phantom
  2. Click SOL balance
  3. Click "Stake"
  4. Choose validator
  5. Enter amount
  6. Confirm
