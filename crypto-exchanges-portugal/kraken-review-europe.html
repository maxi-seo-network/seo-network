What is Kraken?

Kraken was founded in 2011 and is one of the oldest cryptocurrency exchanges still operating. Based in the United States, it's known for:

  • Strong security practices
  • Regulatory compliance in US and Europe
  • Wide range of cryptocurrencies
  • Advanced trading features
  • Free EUR deposits/withdrawals for Europeans

Kraken for European Users

Advantages for Europe:
