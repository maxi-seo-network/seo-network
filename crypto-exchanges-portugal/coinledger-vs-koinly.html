Overview: What Are CoinLedger and Koinly?

CoinLedger

CoinLedger (formerly known as CoinTracker in earlier iterations) is a cryptocurrency tax software platform founded in 2018. The platform has grown to serve over 700,000 crypto investors and has processed more than $70 billion in transactions, according to the company's official website.

CoinLedger automatically imports cryptocurrency transactions from exchanges, wallets, and DeFi protocols, calculates capital gains and losses, and generates tax reports compatible with tax authorities in multiple countries. It's an official TurboTax partner, making it particularly popular among US taxpayers.

Koinly

Koinly is another leading crypto tax platform that supports 800+ exchanges and wallets. It's particularly strong in international tax support, with dedicated templates for countries including Portugal, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, the UK, Australia, and many others.

Koinly emphasizes its country-specific tax reporting, making it a popular choice for European users who need reports that comply with local regulations. The platform supports various cost basis methods including FIFO, LIFO, HIFO, and country-specific rules like Germany's one-year holding period exemption.
