What is Ledger Wallet (Formerly Ledger Live)?

Ledger Wallet represents the evolution of Ledger's cryptocurrency management software, rebranding from "Ledger Live" to better reflect its comprehensive capabilities. Security Architecture

Clear Signing vs. Blind Signing

One of Ledger Wallet's most significant security innovations is its commitment to clear signing. Understanding this distinction is crucial for anyone serious about cryptocurrency security.

Blind signing occurs when you authorize a transaction without fully understanding what it does. Software wallets often present cryptic hexadecimal data that users blindly approve. This practice has led to countless phishing losses and smart contract exploits.

Clear signing means the Ledger device's trusted display shows you exactly what you're approving in human-readable format:

  • Exact token amounts and types being transferred
  • Destination addresses (verified on-screen)
  • Smart contract interactions with decoded parameters
  • NFT transfer details

Ledger's App Catalog includes integrated Web3 applications that support clear signing. When you use these apps through Ledger Wallet, you get the convenience of DeFi interactions with the security of hardware verification.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Ledger Live and Ledger Wallet?

Ledger Wallet is the new name for what was previously called Ledger Live. The rebranding reflects the app's expanded capabilities beyond just portfolio viewing. All features remain available, and existing users were seamlessly transitioned to the new interface.

Can I use Ledger Wallet without a Ledger device?

You can use Ledger Wallet in view-only mode to track your portfolio. However, to sign transactions (send, swap, stake), you need a Ledger hardware wallet. This requirement is fundamental to the security model that makes Ledger Wallet unique.

Is Ledger Wallet available in Portugal?

Yes, Ledger Wallet is available worldwide, including Portugal. Ledger ships devices to Portugal, and the app supports Portuguese language and EUR transactions through integrated payment providers.

How do I update Ledger Wallet?

The app checks for updates automatically. For the desktop version, you can also manually check by navigating to Settings > About > Check for Updates. Mobile versions update through your device's app store.

What happens if I lose my Ledger device?

Your cryptocurrency remains safe on the blockchain. Simply purchase a new Ledger device and restore your wallet using the 24-word recovery phrase you created during initial setup. This is why securely storing your seed phrase is absolutely critical.
