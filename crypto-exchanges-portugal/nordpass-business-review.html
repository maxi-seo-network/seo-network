What Is NordPass Business?

NordPass Business Security Features

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

NordPass Business uses a zero-knowledge architecture. Your master password never leaves your device, and all encryption happens locally. This means:

  • NordPass cannot read your passwords—even if compelled
  • Server breaches expose only encrypted data
  • Complete privacy for sensitive credentials

XChaCha20-Poly1305 Encryption

NordPass uses XChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption—considered more secure than the AES-256 standard used by many competitors. Combined with Argon2 key derivation, this provides:

  • Stronger resistance to brute-force attacks
  • Better performance on mobile devices
  • Modern cryptography standards

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NordPass Business HIPAA compliant?

Yes, NordPass Business meets HIPAA requirements for protecting electronic protected health information (ePHI). The platform includes a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for healthcare organizations.

Can team members have personal password vaults?

Yes. Each team member receives a free Premium personal account alongside their Business account. Personal vaults remain private, while business credentials stay in shared vaults.

What happens when an employee leaves?

Administrators can immediately revoke access and transfer the departing employee's shared credentials to another team member. Activity logs show which credentials were accessed before departure.

Does NordPass Business work offline?

Yes. NordPass stores an encrypted copy of credentials locally. You can access passwords offline, though changes won't sync until you reconnect to the internet.

Can I try NordPass Business before committing?

Yes. NordPass offers a free trial for Business and Enterprise plans—no credit card required. Test all features with your team before purchasing.

How many devices can team members use?

Business and Enterprise plans allow unlimited devices per user. Team members can access their vaults from desktop, mobile, browser, and tablet simultaneously.

Does NordPass Business support API keys?

Yes. Store API keys in secure notes with the same encryption as passwords. Teams can share API keys through shared folders without revealing the actual values.

What's the difference between shared folders and shared items?

Shared folders allow team-wide access to credentials organized by category (e.g., "Marketing Accounts"). Shared items let you share specific credentials with selected team members. Both maintain encryption while enabling collaboration.
