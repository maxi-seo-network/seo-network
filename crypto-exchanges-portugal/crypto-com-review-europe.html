Crypto.com Exchange (Advanced Trading)

The Crypto.com Exchange is for active traders who want professional trading tools:

  • Margin trading: Up to 10x leverage (where available)

  • API access: For algorithmic trading

Recommendation: For Portuguese traders making regular trades, use the Exchange for lower fees. The App is best for beginners, occasional purchases, and card benefits.
