Security Tips for Solana Investors

Protecting your Solana investment requires a proactive approach to security. Follow these essential practices to safeguard your assets.

Exchange Security Best Practices

  • Enable 2FA: Always use two-factor authentication (preferably authenticator app, not SMS)
  • Dedicated Email: Create a unique email address solely for your crypto accounts
  • Strong Passwords: Use a password manager to generate complex, unique passwords
  • Phishing Awareness: Bookmark the official exchange URL and never click links from emails
  • Withdrawal Limits: Set daily withdrawal limits if your exchange offers this feature

Personal Wallet Security

For amounts over €500-€1,000, we strongly recommend transferring your Solana to a personal wallet:

  • Hardware Wallets: Store large amounts on devices like Ledger or Trezor—they never expose your private keys to the internet
  • Software Wallets: For smaller amounts, use wallets like Phantom or Solflare
  • Seed Phrase Protection: Write your recovery phrase on paper and store it in multiple secure locations—never save it digitally
  • Avoid Browser Extensions: Be cautious of wallet extensions that haven't been thoroughly vetted

Recognizing Scams

The cryptocurrency space attracts scammers. Protect yourself by:

  • Never sharing your seed phrase—legitimate services will never ask
  • Being skeptical of "guaranteed returns" or "too good to be true" opportunities
  • Verifying all URLs before entering credentials
  • Ignoring unsolicited messages offering crypto "help" or "support"
