  6. Wait 1-2 business days for funds to arrive (instant with some banks)

Step 4: Buy Solana

  4. Review the exchange rate and fee breakdown

Security Tips for European Solana Investors

Protecting your Solana investment requires a proactive approach to security. Follow these essential practices to safeguard your assets.

Exchange Security Best Practices

  • Enable 2FA: Always use two-factor authentication (preferably authenticator app, not SMS) on your exchange account
  • Use a Dedicated Email: Create a unique email address solely for your crypto accounts
  • Strong Passwords: Use a password manager to generate and store complex, unique passwords
  • Phishing Awareness: Bookmark the official exchange URL and never click links from emails
  • Withdrawal Limits: Set daily withdrawal limits if your exchange offers this feature

Personal Wallet Security

For amounts over €500-€1,000, we strongly recommend transferring your Solana to a personal wallet:

  • Hardware Wallets: Store large amounts on devices like Ledger or Trezor—they never expose your private keys to the internet
  • Software Wallets: For smaller amounts or frequent trading, use wallets like Phantom or Solflare
  • Seed Phrase Protection: Write your recovery phrase on paper and store it in multiple secure locations—never save it digitally or photograph it
  • Avoid Browser Extensions: Be cautious of browser wallet extensions that haven't been thoroughly vetted

Recognizing Scams

The cryptocurrency space attracts scammers. Protect yourself by:

  • Never sharing your seed phrase or private keys with anyone—legitimate services will never ask
  • Being skeptical of "guaranteed returns" or "too good to be true" investment opportunities
  • Verifying all URLs before entering credentials—scammers create convincing fake websites
  • Ignoring unsolicited messages offering crypto "help" or "support"
  • Avoiding clicking links in crypto-related emails or social media messages
