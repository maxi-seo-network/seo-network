Frequently Asked Questions

Which exchange is safest?

Kraken and Coinbase have the strongest security records. Binance also has good security but suffered a hack in 2019 (users were compensated).

Can I use multiple exchanges?

Yes. Many traders use multiple exchanges to access different coins, compare prices, and diversify risk.

Do I need to verify my identity?

Yes. All regulated exchanges require KYC (Know Your Customer) verification with ID and proof of address.

What happens if an exchange goes bankrupt?

It depends. Some exchanges have insurance (like Binance's SAFU fund), but your crypto may not be fully protected. Consider withdrawing to a hardware wallet for large amounts.

Conclusion

For most users, Binance offers the best combination of low fees, coin selection, and features. If you prioritize security and regulation, Kraken is excellent. Beginners may prefer Coinbase despite higher fees.
