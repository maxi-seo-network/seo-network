What is Trezor Suite?

Trezor Suite is the official desktop and mobile application developed by Trezor for managing your hardware wallet. Unlike third-party wallet apps, Trezor Suite is built specifically for Trezor devices, ensuring seamless integration and optimal security.

The app serves as your command center for everything crypto. You can view your portfolio, send and receive transactions, buy and sell cryptocurrencies, swap between assets, stake for rewards, and access DeFi applications—all while your private keys remain safely locked inside your Trezor Safe 3 or Trezor Safe 5.

Desktop vs Mobile: What's the Difference?

Desktop version offers the complete feature set: buying, selling, staking, swapping, sending, receiving, and advanced security settings. It's available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Mobile version brings core functionality to iOS and Android. The March 2026 update (v26.3.2) added fiat value display during trading and improved the interface significantly. Trezor Safe 7 offers full iOS compatibility via Bluetooth, while other models have limited iOS support focused on portfolio viewing and receiving funds.

For wireless connections, Trezor Safe 7 uses Trezor Host Protocol to encrypt every command and transaction. Whether using Bluetooth or USB-C, your communication remains secure from interception or tampering.

Buying and Selling Crypto

Trezor Suite makes it easy to move from fiat to crypto and back. The platform integrates with multiple providers, automatically comparing rates to find you the best deal.

How to Buy Crypto

  1. Connect your Trezor device and open Trezor Suite
  2. Navigate to the "Trade" section
  3. Select "Buy" and choose your cryptocurrency
  4. Enter the amount and compare provider rates
  5. Complete KYC with your chosen provider (varies by region)
  6. Receive crypto directly to your Trezor wallet

How to Sell Crypto

The process works in reverse. Select your crypto, choose the amount to sell, compare provider rates, and receive fiat to your bank account. The country picker was refined in March 2026 to make regional selection smoother—U.S. users can now also select their specific state.

Staking allows you to earn rewards while helping secure blockchain networks. With Trezor Suite, you can stake without giving up custody of your assets—a significant advantage over exchange staking.

Swapping Cryptocurrencies

The Swap feature lets you exchange one crypto for another without using a centralized exchange. Trezor Suite connects to both DEXs and CEXs, showing you the best available rates.
