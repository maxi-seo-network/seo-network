Why Europeans Need a VPN

Privacy & Data Retention

EU data retention laws require ISPs to log your online activity for 6-24 months. A VPN prevents your ISP from seeing what you do online, protecting your privacy.

Crypto Trading

Some crypto exchanges restrict access from certain countries. A VPN lets you connect through a different location to access your accounts while traveling.

Streaming

Access Netflix US, BBC iPlayer (UK), and other streaming services while in Europe. A VPN masks your location.

Public WiFi Security

Working from cafes, airports, or hotels in Europe? Public WiFi is dangerous. A VPN encrypts your connection against hackers.

Price Discrimination

Airlines, hotels, and services often charge different prices based on location. A VPN can help you see prices from other regions.
