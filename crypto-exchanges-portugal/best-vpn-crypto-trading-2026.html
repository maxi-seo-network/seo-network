Why Crypto Traders Need a VPN

Before we compare VPNs, understand why they matter for crypto:

Frequently Asked Questions

Is using a VPN for crypto trading legal?

Yes, using a VPN is legal in most countries. However, some exchanges prohibit VPN use in their terms of service. Check your exchange's policies. Using a VPN to bypass geo-restrictions may violate terms but is rarely enforced.

Can exchanges detect VPN use?

Yes, exchanges can detect VPNs by checking IP addresses against VPN databases. NordVPN's obfuscated servers make detection harder by hiding VPN traffic.

Will a VPN slow down my trading?

VPNs add latency, but with fast services like NordVPN, the difference is usually 10-50ms. For most traders, this is imperceptible. For HFT (high-frequency trading), VPNs may not be suitable.

Should I use a VPN on mobile for crypto?

Yes, especially on public WiFi. NordVPN and Surfshark have excellent mobile apps. Always enable the kill switch on mobile to prevent IP leaks.

Can I use a free VPN for crypto?

We strongly advise against free VPNs for crypto. Free VPNs often log your data, inject ads, and may sell your browsing history. Your financial data is too sensitive for free services.
