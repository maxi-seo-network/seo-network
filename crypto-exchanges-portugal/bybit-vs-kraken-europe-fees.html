Fee Structure Breakdown: Understanding the Basics

Before diving into the numbers, it's essential to understand what fees you'll encounter on both platforms. Cryptocurrency exchanges typically charge two types of fees:

Spot Trading Fees

Spot trading fees apply when buying or selling cryptocurrencies directly. Most exchanges use a maker-taker model:

  • Maker fees apply when you place limit orders that add liquidity to the order book (your order doesn't execute immediately).
  • Taker fees apply when you place market orders that remove liquidity (your order executes immediately against existing orders).
