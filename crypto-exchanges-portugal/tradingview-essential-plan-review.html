The Volume Profile Decision Point

If your trading strategy depends on Volume Profile—the institutional order flow tool that reveals where heavy trading occurred at specific prices—Plus is your minimum tier. Essential doesn't include it. Period.

For crypto traders using support and resistance based on volume clusters, or those analyzing whale accumulation patterns, Volume Profile is non-negotiable. Essential cannot serve these traders.

The Multi-Timeframe Decision Point

Essential's 2-chart limit is sufficient for strategies using 2 timeframes (e.g., daily for trend, 4-hour for entries). But if you analyze with 3+ timeframes—say daily, 4-hour, 1-hour, and 15-minute simultaneously—you need Plus's 4-chart capability.

Consider your typical analysis: How many charts do you need visible at once? If the answer is 2, Essential works. If it's 3 or more, Plus becomes necessary.
