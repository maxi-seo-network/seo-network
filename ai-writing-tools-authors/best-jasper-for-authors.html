What is Jasper AI and Why Should Authors Care?

Jasper AI (formerly Jarvis) is a generative AI platform built specifically for marketing and content creation. For authors, it's become an invaluable tool for drafting manuscripts, creating outlines, overcoming writer's block, and maintaining consistent voice throughout long-form projects.

Unlike generic AI chatbots, Jasper AI is designed with authors in mind. Its Brand Voice feature allows you to train the AI on your writing style, ensuring generated content matches your unique voice. This is crucial for authors who need consistency across chapters, series, or multiple books.

With over 100,000 businesses using Jasper—including major publishers and independent authors—it's become one of the most trusted AI writing assistants for serious writers. The platform offers 50+ templates designed for various content types, including specialized templates for long-form content, blog posts, and creative writing.

Key Features for Authors

1. Long-Form Content Generation

Jasper's flagship feature for authors is its ability to generate coherent, long-form content. Unlike basic chatbots that produce disjointed paragraphs, Jasper maintains context across thousands of words—essential for book chapters, articles, and detailed guides.

How authors use it: Draft chapters, expand outlines, create character backstories, write scene descriptions, and generate dialogue that fits your characters' personalities.

2. Brand Voice (Style Consistency)

This is Jasper's killer feature for authors. Train the AI on your writing samples, and it will learn your voice, tone, and style preferences. This ensures that AI-generated content matches your existing work—crucial for authors writing series or maintaining a consistent author brand.

Setup: Upload writing samples, define tone rules (formal, conversational, humorous, etc.), and set formatting preferences. Jasper then applies these rules to all generated content.

3. Knowledge Assets

Upload your research materials, character bibles, world-building documents, or reference texts. Jasper uses this knowledge to generate content that's accurate and consistent with your established universe.

For fiction authors: Upload character descriptions, setting details, and plot outlines to keep AI-generated content consistent.

For non-fiction authors: Upload research papers, interview transcripts, and source materials for factually accurate content.

4. AI Templates

Jasper offers 50+ templates designed for various writing tasks:

  • Blog Post Intro: Hook readers from the first paragraph
  • Content Improver: Rewrite existing content for better flow
  • Explain It To A Child: Simplify complex concepts
  • Feature to Benefit: Transform features into compelling benefits
  • AIDA Framework: Attention-Interest-Desire-Action for persuasive writing
  • PAS Framework: Problem-Agitate-Solution for copywriting

5. Jasper Art (AI Image Generation)

Create custom artwork for book covers, chapter headers, character portraits, and marketing materials. Includes background replacement, upscaling, and composition tools.

How to Get Started with Jasper AI

Step 1: Start Your Free Trial

Sign up for the 7-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card required. You'll have full access to all features including Brand Voice and Knowledge Assets.

Step 2: Train Your Brand Voice

  1. Go to Brand Voice in settings
  2. Upload 3-5 samples of your writing (10,000+ words total)
  3. Describe your style (conversational, formal, humorous, etc.)
  4. Add formatting preferences
  5. Test by generating sample content and refining

Step 3: Upload Knowledge Assets

For authors, this is crucial. Upload:

  • Character descriptions and relationships
  • Setting descriptions and maps
  • Plot outlines and timelines
  • Research materials and source documents
  • Style guide and tone preferences

Step 4: Start Creating

Use the Canvas for long-form content or select templates for specific tasks. Start with the Content Improver template to enhance existing paragraphs, or use the Long-Form Assistant for new content.

How much does Jasper AI cost per month for authors?

Jasper AI costs $59/month (billed annually) or $69/month (billed monthly) for the Pro plan. Business plans with unlimited seats and advanced features are custom-priced based on team size and needs.

Can Jasper AI write an entire book?

Jasper AI can help write significant portions of a book, but works best as a collaborative tool. Authors typically use it for generating chapters, expanding outlines, overcoming writer's block, and editing. Most successful authors use Jasper as an AI co-writer rather than a complete replacement.

Does Jasper AI offer a free trial for authors?

Yes, Jasper AI offers a 7-day free trial of the Pro plan. Authors can test all features including long-form content generation, brand voice, and templates without commitment. No credit card required to start.

Which is better for authors: Jasper AI or ChatGPT?

Jasper AI is better specifically for authors due to its focus on long-form content, brand voice consistency, and marketing-specific templates. ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) is more versatile and cheaper, but Jasper's Pro plan ($59/mo) offers dedicated authoring features, document collaboration, and marketing integrations that serious authors find valuable.
