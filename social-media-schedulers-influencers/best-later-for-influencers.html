Later for Influencers: Complete 2026 Guide to Plans, Pricing & Features

The visual-first scheduler built for Instagram creators. Compare plans, pricing, and find the right Later package for your influencer business.

Is Later Worth It for Influencers in 2026?

Later has built its reputation as the visual-first social media scheduler. Founded in 2014 as an Instagram-focused tool, it has expanded to TikTok, Pinterest, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn—but Instagram remains its strength. For influencers who prioritize visual planning, aesthetic feed curation, and Instagram-focused growth, Later offers features that competitors can't match.

The drag-and-drop grid preview shows exactly how your Instagram feed will look before you post. The Linkin.bio feature creates a clickable version of your feed for driving traffic. The Stories storyboard lets you plan visual narratives. These Instagram-specific tools make Later the top choice for visual creators—even at higher price points than Buffer or Hootsuite.

In this comprehensive guide, we break down Later's pricing, plans, and real-world performance for influencer workflows.

Who Should Consider Later?

Later is ideal for influencers who:

  • Post primarily to Instagram and TikTok
  • Need visual grid planning for aesthetic feeds
  • Want a link-in-bio tool without extra cost
  • Value mobile-first editing and planning
  • Create Pinterest content (excellent Pinterest support)
  • Want a genuinely useful free plan

Who Should Choose Alternatives?

Consider Buffer, Hootsuite, or Planoly instead if:

  • You need YouTube scheduling (Later doesn't support it)
  • You manage 5+ platforms including LinkedIn heavily
  • You need unified inbox management for comments/DMs
  • You're an agency managing multiple clients
  • You need team approval workflows

What's a "Social Set"?

Later counts "social sets"—one social set = one Instagram account, one TikTok account, one Pinterest account, etc. across all supported platforms. This is different from Buffer (which counts each profile) or Hootsuite (which counts each account separately).

Example: If you're an influencer with one Instagram account, one TikTok account, and one Pinterest account, that's 1 social set. If you manage your personal brand and a side business, you'd need 2 social sets (requires Advanced plan).

Annual vs Monthly Billing

  • Growth: $40/month (annual) or ~$48/month (monthly)

🆓 Later Free Plan: Best Free Tier for Instagram Creators

Free Plan Overview

Price: $0 forever

Best For: Micro-influencers, testers, Instagram-focused beginners

What You Get

  • 1 Social Set: Connect Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn (all platforms for 1 account each)
  • 10 Posts Per Platform: 60 total scheduled posts across all platforms
  • Instagram Grid Preview: Visual planning for your feed
  • Stories Scheduling: Plan and schedule Instagram Stories
  • Linkin.bio: Basic link-in-bio landing page (Later branded)
  • Basic Analytics: Post performance and engagement metrics
  • Mobile App: Full-featured iOS and Android apps

Limitations

  • 10 Posts Per Platform: If you post daily to Instagram, you'll hit this limit in 10 days
  • Later Branding: Linkin.bio shows "Powered by Later"
  • No TikTok Auto-Publish: Manual publishing required
  • No AI Features: No content ideas or hashtag suggestions
  • 1 User Only: Can't add team members
  • Basic Analytics Only: Limited insights compared to paid plans

When Free Plan Works

The free plan is genuinely useful for:

  • Micro-influencers posting 2-3 times per week
  • Testing Later before committing to paid
  • Instagram creators who need grid preview without paying
  • Those starting out with social media scheduling

When to Upgrade: If you post more than 10 times per platform per month, need TikTok auto-publishing, or want AI content suggestions, upgrade to Growth ($40/month) for unlimited posts.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Later free for influencers?

Yes, Later offers a free plan that includes 1 social set (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn), 10 posts per platform per month, grid preview, and Linkin.bio. This works for micro-influencers or those testing the platform. For active creators posting daily, the Growth plan ($40/month) with unlimited posts is more practical.

How much does Later cost in 2026?

Later pricing in 2026: Free (1 social set, 10 posts/platform), Starter ($15/month, 30 posts/platform), Growth ($40/month, unlimited posts), Advanced ($80/month, 7 social sets, team features). Annual billing saves 17-20%. For most influencers, Growth at $40/month provides the best value with unlimited posts and AI features.

Is Later good for Instagram influencers?

Yes, Later is excellent for Instagram influencers. Its visual grid preview is the best in the industry, allowing you to drag-and-drop posts and see exactly how your feed will look. The Linkin.bio feature creates a clickable version of your Instagram feed for driving traffic. For Instagram-focused creators, Later's visual planning tools justify the subscription cost.

Can Later schedule TikTok posts?

Yes, Later supports TikTok scheduling with auto-publishing on all paid plans (Starter, Growth, Advanced). You can upload videos, add captions and hashtags, see trending sound suggestions, and schedule posts to publish automatically. Later's TikTok integration is polished and one of the best among schedulers.

Does Later support YouTube?

No, Later does not support YouTube scheduling. This is Later's biggest limitation for multi-platform creators. If you create YouTube content, you'll need a separate scheduler like Buffer or Hootsuite, or use YouTube Studio's native scheduling. For YouTube creators, Buffer ($6/channel/month) is a better choice.

What's Later Linkin.bio?

Linkin.bio is Later's free link-in-bio tool. It creates a clickable version of your Instagram feed where each post links to a URL you specify (blog post, product page, affiliate link, etc.). This is included free with all Later plans. Paid plans remove the "Powered by Later" branding. It's similar to Linktree but integrated with your Later scheduling.

Can I add team members to Later?

Team features are only available on the Advanced plan ($80/month), which includes up to 6 team members. The Free, Starter, and Growth plans are limited to 1 user. If you work with an assistant or manager, you'll need the Advanced plan—or consider Buffer ($12/channel/month for team features) or Hootsuite ($249/month for team collaboration).

Is Later better than Buffer?

Later is better for Instagram-focused creators who need visual grid planning. Buffer is better for budget-conscious creators who need YouTube scheduling or transparent per-channel pricing. Later's grid preview and Linkin.bio make it superior for Instagram. Buffer's YouTube support and $6/channel pricing make it better for multi-platform creators. Choose based on your platform priorities.
